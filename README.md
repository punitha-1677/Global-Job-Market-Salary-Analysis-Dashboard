 Global-Job-Market-Salary-Analysis-Dashboard
 
Global Job Market Salary Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Tableau project designed to analyze salary trends across various job roles, industries, experience levels, company sizes, and countries. The dashboard transforms raw job market data into meaningful visual insights, helping users understand the factors that influence salaries worldwide.

Objectives
Analyze average and highest salaries across different job roles.
Compare salary trends based on experience level.
Explore salary distribution by industry.
Analyze company size and its impact on salaries.
Understand remote work distribution.
Visualize salary information across different countries.
Create an interactive dashboard with filters for better analysis.

Key Insights
AI Engineer has one of the highest average salaries.
Enterprise companies generally offer higher salaries than startups.
Senior professionals earn significantly more than freshers.
Salary differences across industries are relatively small.
Remote, Hybrid, and On-site jobs are distributed almost equally.
Salary trends vary across countries.
